Ukiyo-e Guinomi 浮世絵ぐい呑（富嶽三十六景）
φ8.5cm × H4.5cm／約40g｜各図柄5点限定（Early Bird 各2／KS Special 各3）
A Wajima-nuri sake cup with one scene from Hokusai's Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji on its interior. Gold and silver are laid in maki-e over a durable lacquer base, then finished with colored lacquer — light, easy to hold, gentle on the lips.
（内側に北斎の富嶽三十六景。蒔絵で金銀を施し色漆で仕上げ。軽く持ちやすく口当たりやわらか。）

図柄ごとの英語紹介（5種）：

  • The Great Wave off Kanagawa（神奈川沖浪裏）— The most famous wave in the world, curling above three boats as Fuji watches from afar.
  • Kajikazawa in Kai Province（甲州石班澤）— A lone fisherman casting his net, Fuji rising calm beyond — a meditation on patience.
  • South Wind, Clear Sky / Red Fuji（凱風快晴）— The mountain glowing crimson at dawn, the most auspicious of Hokusai's views.
  • Fuji through the Owari Province（尾州不二見原）— Fuji framed inside a cooper's giant barrel — the everyday and the eternal in one circle.
  • Rainstorm Beneath the Summit（山下白雨）— Lightning splits the dark slopes while the summit stands serene.
